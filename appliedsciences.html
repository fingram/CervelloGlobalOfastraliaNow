Applied Sciences



The Appied Sciences Division develops and applies a fast, agile, and robust methodology to accomplish Operational Assessments on emerging capabilities and Quick Reaction Tests (QRT) on Tactics, Techniques and Procedures (TTP).





The Applied Sciences Division's objective is to research innovative new wargame scenarios, experiments, and table top exercises that can be used by military and government entities, components, and stakeholders to ananlyze issues across mssion areas. An additional recommendations in support of devising and aquiring a comprehensive suite of modeling and simulation capabilities to enhance the ability to efficiently and effectively plan and execute operations and advocate for capabilities as directed by authoritistive guidance.





Modeling and simulation (M&S) capabilities will be used to support strategic analysis of current and future capabilites; wargames and eperimentaion; and analysis and recommendations for development and improved Concept of Operations, tactics, techniques and procedures, and technology roadmaps. These activities will identify gaps in mission capablities or explore options to address identified capability shortfalls. These capabilities provide opportunities to define and refine mission-related polocies,tactics, techniqes, and procedures, organizational constructs, and effectiveness of operational tools throughout the community of intrest.
